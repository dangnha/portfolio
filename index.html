About

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at University of Example. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning and natural language processing, with a particular focus on developing interpretable models for complex reasoning tasks.

Prior to joining University of Example, I completed my Ph.D. at Stanford University (2016) and was a postdoctoral researcher at MIT CSAIL. My work has been recognized with an NSF CAREER Award, a Google Faculty Research Award, and best paper awards at NeurIPS and ACL.
